Professional Summary

Dedicated to Student Growth, Confidence, and Lifelong Learning

I am a licensed North Carolina Mathematics Educator with a Bachelor of Science and Education in Mathematics and a Master of Science in Information Technology. My passion is helping students develop confidence, curiosity, and strong problem-solving skills through engaging instruction and meaningful support.

Serving as a Learning Coach within North Carolina's virtual public school system has given me valuable experience supporting students, communicating with teachers, monitoring academic progress, and reinforcing mathematics instruction in an online learning environment.

I believe every student can succeed when provided with encouragement, individualized instruction, high expectations, and opportunities to learn from mistakes while developing independence and resilience.

Teaching Philosophy

Every Student Can Learn Mathematics

I believe every student is capable of learning mathematics when provided with encouragement, clear instruction, and opportunities to grow through perseverance. Learning becomes meaningful when students feel supported, challenged, and confident enough to ask questions and learn from mistakes.

My goal is to create a classroom environment where students develop problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and confidence while recognizing that success comes through consistent effort and determination.

Whether teaching in a traditional classroom or a virtual learning environment, I strive to build positive relationships, maintain high expectations, and help every student discover their potential.
